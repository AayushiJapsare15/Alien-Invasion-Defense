FAQs

How do I start the game?

Click the "Start" button to begin the game.

How do I move and shoot in the game?

You can use your keyboard arrow keys or on-screen controls to move your spaceship and shoot at alien ships.

How many lives do I have?

You have 3 lives. You lose a life if an alien reaches your base or if your ship is hit.

What happens when I lose all my lives?

When you lose all 3 lives, the game ends. Click "Restart" to try again.

How do I progress in the game?

To progress, you must destroy all alien ships in each wave to move on to the next level and increase your score.

Where can I find more information about the game?

Refer to the game instructions for detailed gameplay rules and tips.
